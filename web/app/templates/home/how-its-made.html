How It's Made

From public records to visualization. This is how the website is made.

Collect Public Data

We periodically browse the publically available companies records from the VFSC and gather a copy of the data. The data we gather includes the following:

  • Company details including addresses, filings and more.
  • Shareholder details and number of shares.
  • Director details.

Parse and Organize

We then parse the raw data and organize into our database.

  • Review new and old companies.
  • Match shareholders and directors to known companies or individuals.

Graph The Data

Lastly, we visualize the data and present it to you here.
